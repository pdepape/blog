Documentation system

A publishing model built for technical accuracy

Strong documentation is more than polished writing. It needs ownership, review loops, product signals, and reusable patterns that scale across every product area.

  1. Map the journey. Identify the moments where a reader is blocked, learning, integrating, or making a decision.
  2. Design the source of truth. Separate conceptual guidance, task flows, references, and release artifacts.
  3. Maintain the loop. Use product changes, search logs, support tickets, and analytics to keep docs fresh.
